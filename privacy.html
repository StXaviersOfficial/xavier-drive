Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2025  ·  St. Xavier's School

Overview

XavierDrive is a school file-sharing and management platform built for St. Xavier's School. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

By using XavierDrive, you agree to the practices described in this policy. This app is intended for use by students, teachers, and staff of St. Xavier's School only.

Information We Collect

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google Account.
  • Google Drive Data: With your permission, we access files in your Google Drive to enable file uploads, downloads, and sharing features within the platform.
  • Usage Data: We may collect basic usage information such as which features you use, to improve the app experience.
  • Locally Stored Data: Some settings (like timetable data and announcements) are stored in your browser's local storage on your device.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you securely via Google Sign-In.
  • To enable file upload, viewing, and sharing through Google Drive.
  • To display your name and profile picture within the app interface.
  • To provide role-based access (Student / Teacher) to appropriate features.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

Google API Services

XavierDrive uses the following Google API scopes:

  • openid, profile, email — To identify you and display your profile.
  • drive.file — To upload and manage files created by this app in your Drive.
  • drive.readonly — To read and display files shared within the school platform.

Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Data Storage & Security

  • Files are stored in Google Drive and governed by Google's security infrastructure.
  • We do not operate our own servers or databases that store your files.
  • Access tokens are stored in browser memory and cleared when you sign out.
  • We recommend signing out on shared or public devices.

Children's Privacy

XavierDrive is used by school students, including those under the age of 13. We collect only the minimum information necessary for the platform to function. We do not knowingly collect personal data beyond what Google provides during sign-in, and we do not share student data with third parties.

Your Rights

  • You may revoke XavierDrive's access to your Google Account at any time via Google Account Permissions.
  • You may request deletion of any data associated with your account by contacting us.
  • Signing out of the app clears your session data from the browser.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of XavierDrive after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
